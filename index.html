About Technovate 2025

Technovate 2025 is the premier IEEE Day celebration at Maharishi University of Information Technology (MUIT), Lucknow Campus, set for 7th & 8th October 2025. This two-day tech fest is your chance to push boundaries, showcase innovation, and compete in thrilling technical challenges. Whether you're a coder, designer, or visionary, Technovate is where ideas come to life!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Technovate 2025? +

Technovate 2025 is open to all students from any recognized educational institution. Participants can register individually or in teams, depending on the sub-event.

Is there a registration fee? +

Registration fees vary by sub-event. Please check the registration form for detailed fee information.

What are the prizes for the sub-events? +

Prizes include certificates, trophies, and potential cash rewards for top performers in each sub-event. Detailed prize information will be shared closer to the event date.

Can I participate in multiple sub-events? +

Yes, participants can register for multiple sub-events, provided there are no scheduling conflicts. Check the event schedule for details.

Will accommodation be provided for participants from other colleges? +

Currently, no accommodation is being arranged by the organizing team. Outstation participants are advised to manage their own stay nearby. A list of nearby budget hotels and hostels will be shared upon request.

Will food or refreshments be provided during the event? +

No, food or refreshments will not be provided by the organizing team. Participants are advised to make their own arrangements for meals and water. A canteen is available on campus, and there are several nearby eateries outside the university premises.
